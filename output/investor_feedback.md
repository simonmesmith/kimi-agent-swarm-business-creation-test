- Year 2: 10,056 orders/month
- Year 3: 24,501 orders/month

- Customer service tickets increase 10x (not linear)
- Inventory management gets complex (safety stock, expiry dates)
- Chargebacks increase (scale = more fraud)
- Returns processing becomes a full-time job
- Quality control gets harder

Your fixed OpEx goes from $462K (Year 1) to $785K (Year 3). That's only 70% increase for 13x order volume. **Not realistic.**

Realistic Year 3 OpEx: $1.2-1.5M minimum.
