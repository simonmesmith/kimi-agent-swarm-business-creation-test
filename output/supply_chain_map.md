## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document provides a complete supply chain mapping for a premium liposomal Magnesium L-Threonate supplement with additional sleep-support ingredients (L-theanine, GABA, Apigenin, L-glycine). The analysis covers raw material suppliers, contract manufacturers, cost breakdowns, MOQs, lead times, and risk assessments.

**Key Findings:**
- **Estimated COGS at 10K units:** $8.50-$12.00 per bottle
- **Estimated COGS at 50K units:** $6.00-$8.50 per bottle
- **Estimated COGS at 100K units:** $4.50-$6.50 per bottle
- **Typical MOQ for liposomal liquids:** 2,500-5,000 units
- **Lead time (first order):** 8-12 weeks
