
# NOVA MIND / SYNAPSE / LUMINA
## Brand Design System
### Premium Sleep + Cognitive Supplement

---

## 1. BRAND OVERVIEW

### Positioning
- **Category**: Premium sleep + cognitive wellness supplement
- **Target Audience**: Stressed professionals, ages 30-50, science-minded
- **Price Point**: $49.99/month (premium positioning)
- **Key Attributes**: Trust, Science, Premium, Clean, Modern
- **Brand Essence**: "Technical Magic" - clinically-backed ingredients that feel like magic but work like science

### Brand Names (Options)
1. **NOVA MIND** - Suggests new beginnings, mental clarity, brightness
2. **SYNAPSE** - Directly references neural connections, cognitive function
3. **LUMINA** - Implies illumination, clarity, premium quality

### Color Usage Guidelines
- **Primary (Deep Navy)**: Use for main headings, navigation, trust signals, and primary brand elements
- **Secondary (Teal)**: Use for secondary headings, backgrounds, hover states, and supporting content
- **Accent (Gold)**: Use sparingly for CTAs, key highlights, and premium indicators. Never use for body text.
- **Light (Soft White)**: Primary background color for cards, sections, and clean spaces
- **Dark (Near Black)**: Body text and footer backgrounds
- **Success (Forest Green)**: Positive indicators, checkmarks, natural/health messaging

## 3. TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM

### Heading Font
**Primary**: Montserrat, Inter, or Poppins
- **Weights**: Bold (700), SemiBold (600), Medium (500)
- **H1**: 48-64px, Bold - Hero headlines, major sections
- **H2**: 36-42px, SemiBold - Section headers
- **H3**: 24-28px, Medium - Subsection headers
- **Line Height**: 1.2-1.3
- **Letter Spacing**: -0.5px to 0px

### Body Font
**Primary**: Open Sans, Source Sans Pro, or Roboto
- **Weights**: Regular (400), Medium (500)
- **Body**: 16-18px, Regular - Paragraphs, descriptions
- **Small**: 14px, Regular - Captions, footnotes, legal text
- **Line Height**: 1.5-1.7
- **Letter Spacing**: 0px

### Accent Font
**Primary**: Playfair Display or Merriweather (Serif)
- **Weights**: Italic, Regular
- **Accent**: 18-24px, Italic - Quotes, testimonials, premium feel
- **Usage**: Use sparingly for emphasis and elegance

### Logo Concept 3: LUMINA
- **Style**: Premium badge style with elegant icon
- **Elements**: Circular badge with gold ring, gradient center, brain wave visualization
- **Best For**: Premium, luxury wellness positioning

### Logo Usage Guidelines
- Always maintain clear space around the logo (minimum 20px)
- Use primary color version on light backgrounds
- Use white/inverted version on dark backgrounds
- Never stretch, distort, or rotate the logo
- Minimum size: 100px width for web, 0.5" for print

## 5. BUTTON & CTA STYLES

### Primary CTA
- **Background**: Gold (#C9A227)
- **Text**: White (#FFFFFF)
- **Border Radius**: 8px (web), 30px (pill style for emphasis)
- **Padding**: 16px 32px
- **Font**: 16px, SemiBold, All Caps
- **Hover**: Darken 10%, add subtle shadow

### Secondary CTA
- **Background**: Transparent
- **Text**: Deep Navy (#1E3A5F)
- **Border**: 2px solid Deep Navy
- **Border Radius**: 8px
- **Padding**: 16px 32px
- **Font**: 16px, SemiBold
- **Hover**: Fill with Deep Navy, text becomes white

### Ghost Button
- **Background**: Transparent
- **Text**: Teal (#4A90A4)
- **Border**: None
- **Font**: 14px, Medium
- **Hover**: Underline, slight color darken

## 6. SPACING & LAYOUT

### Section Spacing
- **Large Sections**: 80-120px vertical padding
- **Medium Sections**: 60px vertical padding
- **Small Sections**: 40px vertical padding
- **Content Blocks**: 24-32px internal padding

### Container Widths
- **Full Width**: 100% (with 80px side padding on desktop)
- **Standard**: 1200px max-width
- **Narrow**: 800px max-width (for reading content)

### Grid System
- **12-column grid** for desktop
- **6-column grid** for tablet
- **Single column** for mobile
- **Gutter**: 24px (desktop), 16px (mobile)

## 7. ICONOGRAPHY

### Style Guidelines
- **Line icons** with 2px stroke weight
- **Rounded corners** (2px radius)
- **Consistent sizing**: 24px (standard), 48px (large)
- **Colors**: Primary or Secondary palette

### Recommended Icon Sets
- Phosphor Icons
- Heroicons
- Feather Icons

## 8. IMAGERY GUIDELINES

### Photography Style
- **Clean, minimal backgrounds**
- **Natural lighting** preferred
- **Diverse representation** of professionals
- **Authentic moments** over staged poses
- **Soft, muted color grading** that complements brand palette

### Subject Matter
- Professionals in calm, focused states
- Morning routines and wellness rituals
- Sleep environments (clean, minimal bedrooms)
- Nature elements (suggesting calm and restoration)

### Image Treatment
- Slight desaturation (10-15%)
- Cool color temperature
- Subtle contrast boost
- Avoid heavy filters or effects

## 9. COPY & TONE OF VOICE

### Brand Voice Attributes
- **Scientific but accessible**: Use research-backed claims without jargon
- **Empathetic**: Acknowledge the struggle, offer genuine solutions
- **Confident but not arrogant**: Position as expert guide, not know-it-all
- **Premium but approachable**: High quality without being elitist

### Key Messaging Pillars
1. **Science-Backed**: Clinical research, proven ingredients
2. **Melatonin-Free**: Natural sleep support without dependency
3. **Dual Benefit**: Sleep + cognitive performance in one
4. **Professional-Focused**: Designed for high-performers

### Sample Headlines
- "Wake Up Sharper"
- "Sleep Better. Think Clearer."
- "The Premium Melatonin-Free Formula"
- "Restored Sleep. Enhanced Mind."

## 10. DIGITAL ADVERTISING SPECIFICATIONS

### Facebook/Instagram Feed (1200x628)
- Focus: Problem agitation or solution presentation
- Keep text under 20% of image
- Strong visual hierarchy
- Clear CTA button

### Instagram Square (1080x1080)
- Focus: Benefits, testimonials, or product showcase
- Centered composition
- Bold typography
- Brand mark in corner

### Display Banner (300x250)
- Focus: Urgency and strong CTA
- Minimal text
- High contrast
- Immediate value proposition

---

## 11. ACCESSIBILITY GUIDELINES

### Color Contrast
- **Normal text**: Minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio
- **Large text**: Minimum 3:1 contrast ratio
- **Interactive elements**: Minimum 3:1 contrast ratio

### Typography
- **Minimum body text size**: 16px
- **Line height**: Minimum 1.5 for body text
- **Avoid justified text**: Use left-aligned for readability

### Interactive Elements
- **Focus states**: Visible outline (2px) on all interactive elements
- **Touch targets**: Minimum 44x44px for mobile
